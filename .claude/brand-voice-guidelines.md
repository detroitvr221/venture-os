## Voice Attributes

- **Decisive** — Clear recommendations, not option menus. "We recommend X because..." not "You could choose from..."
- **Execution-Focused** — We do the work, not just the thinking. "Here's what we built" not "Here's what we could build."
- **Warmly Professional** — Confident and strategic, never cold or corporate. Contractions are fine. Slang is not.
- **Premium but Accessible** — High-quality work at approachable pricing. Not cheap, not intimidating.
- **Systems-Minded** — Everything is a running system, not a one-off project. "Operating partner" not "service provider."
- **Transparently Human** — Real people, real execution, real results. We use modern tools, but the client relationship is human.
- **Accountable** — Clear deliverables, monthly check-ins, visible progress. No black boxes.

## Positioning — What We Say

**How to describe Northbridge:**
- "Human-led digital growth"
- "Built with strategy, backed by execution"
- "Web, visibility, and growth support for modern businesses"
- "We build brands, websites, and systems that move businesses forward"
- "From launch to growth, Northbridge helps businesses show up stronger online"

**What NOT to lead with:**
- "AI agency" or "AI-first"
- "AI does everything" or "fully automated"
- "Replace your team"
- "Powered by AI" as the headline

**Instead say:**
- "Our team combines strategy, design, development, and modern systems to help businesses grow faster and operate smarter."
- "Behind the scenes, we use modern tools and automation — but what you see is real people, real execution, real results."

## Writing Principles

1. **Lead with outcomes, follow with how.** "We grew their traffic 340% in 90 days" then explain the approach.
2. **Be specific. Vague claims cost credibility.** "$99/month" beats "affordable." "5 pages" beats "a website."
3. **Structure is a voice element.** Headers, lists, clear sections. Walls of prose are off-brand.
4. **One meeting is all we need — sound like it.** Eliminate filler and over-qualification.
5. **Warm does not mean casual.** Professional services firm that moves fast. Not a startup "disrupting stuff."
6. **Always lead human.** If you mention tools or systems, anchor it to human strategy and oversight.
7. **Accountability is a feature.** Monthly reports, clear deliverables, and visible progress build trust.

## Content Guidelines by Channel

### Website
- Hero: Declarative headline (5-8 words), active voice, outcome-first
- Always lead with the human team, not tools
- CTA: Direct — "Start Growing," "See Packages," "Get Started"
- Show pricing transparently

### Email
- Subject: Under 50 characters, plain language, specific
- Body: Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences), one action per email
- Include: sender identity, unsubscribe, physical address
- Quiet hours: no sends 9PM-8AM in recipient timezone

### Social (LinkedIn primary)
- Format: Problem insight Northbridge approach
- Anchor every claim to a specific outcome or number
- 1-3 hashtags max
- Always human-reviewed before posting

### Proposals
- Structure: Situation Approach Deliverables Investment Next Step
- Present recommendations, not option menus
- Use client's language from discovery
- Reference specific package tier when applicable
