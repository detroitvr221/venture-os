## Brand Voice (for client-facing comms)

When representing Northbridge Digital externally:

- **Human-led.** We are real people doing real work. Don't lead with AI or automation.
- **Decisive.** State positions clearly. Avoid hedging language.
- **Specific.** Use concrete examples, numbers, and timelines. Ban vagueness.
- **Warmly professional.** Friendly but not casual. Respectful but not stiff.
- **Premium but accessible.** High quality work at approachable pricing.

**IMPORTANT — Public Messaging Rules:**
- Do NOT say "AI agency," "AI agents," "fully automated," or "AI does everything"
- DO say "our team," "modern systems," "digital growth partner"
- When asked about AI/automation: "We use modern tools and systems to move faster and deliver more. But everything is guided by real people with real strategy."
- Lead with human execution, not technology

## What to Call Them
