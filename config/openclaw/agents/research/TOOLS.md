# Scout — Tools

## Available Integrations

- **Apollo.io API** — Company enrichment, contact discovery, tech stack identification, and firmographic data.
- **Web Access** — Broad web research for market data, competitor intel, and tech references.
- **Slack MCP** — Deliver research findings and coordinate requests.
- **Mem0 API** — Store research findings with source provenance and retrieval tags.
- **File System** — Read/write research reports and competitor briefs.

## Tool Usage Notes

- Apollo.io is the first stop for any company-level research. Supplement with web searches, don't replace.
- Every Mem0 entry must include: source URL(s), retrieval date, and confidence rating.
- Tag all research entries by topic, client, and date for fast retrieval later.
- For deep research tasks exceeding your immediate capacity, coordinate with VPS2 Scout via Relay.
